About us

About us

All women can embrace themselves, define their future, and change the world.

Welcome to Women in Sustainable STEM Africa, (WiSSA), a community aimed at empowering women to shape a sustainable future through STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields across Africa. We are passionate about promoting gender equality, sustainability, and innovation in STEM fields, and we believe that by harnessing the talents and potential of women, we can create a brighter and more sustainable future for Africa and beyond.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

We are dedicated to supporting, advocating for, and celebrating women in sustainable STEM fields. Our mission is to promote diversity and inclusivity within STEM, ensuring that women have access to resources and opportunities to thrive in these critical fields. Through initiatives focused on mentorship, education, and community-building, we are committed to cultivating a network of empowered women who are at the forefront of driving positive change towards a more sustainable future.

By fostering mentorship, providing educational support, and fostering a sense of community, we aim to create an environment where women in STEM can flourish. Our efforts are focused on empowering women to take on leadership roles and make meaningful contributions to sustainability. Together, we are building a strong network of women who are leading the charge towards a more inclusive, diverse, and sustainable future in STEM.
